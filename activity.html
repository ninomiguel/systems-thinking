What I have learned in systems thinking?


Systems thinking helps us see the world as a connected whole, not a collection of separate things. It shows us how the parts of a system interact with each other and how changes in one part can affect other parts. It also helps us understand the role of feedback loops, time delays, and boundaries in systems. Using tools like diagrams and simulations, systems thinking can be used to solve problems and improve systems.
