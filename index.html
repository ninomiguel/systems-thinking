Introduction to Systems Thinking

What is Systems Thinking?

Systems thinking is a way of understanding how things are connected and how they work together as a whole.

What is a System?

A system is a group of things that work together to achieve a common goal.

What is a Systems Perspective?

The systems perspective is a way of seeing the world as a connected whole, rather than a collection of separate objects.
